# Zeenat-AI (زینت) 🛡️
### The Multi-Agent Guardian & Empowerment Swarm for Pakistani Women

**Zeenat-AI** is a sophisticated, AI-driven protection and empowerment platform designed specifically for the unique legal, social, and religious landscape of Pakistan. It uses a "Swarm Intelligence" model to provide comprehensive audits of a woman's rights, safety, and independence.

---

## 🌟 Mission
To empower women in Pakistan by providing instant, accessible, and accurate legal and Sharia-based knowledge, connecting them with life-saving resources, and auditing their specific situations for rights violations.

---

## 🤖 The Swarm Agents
Zeenat-AI operates through specialized agents that analyze user input in parallel:

1.  **⚖️ The Advocate**: Analyzes Pakistani Civil and Criminal Law (PPC, MFLO 1961, Protection of Women Acts). It identifies specific legal breaches (e.g., Section 498A for inheritance deprivation).
2.  **🕌 Sharia Expert**: Provides religious perspectives on marriage (Nikkah), inheritance (Wives/Daughters shares), and mobility, citing traditional and modern Islamic sources.
3.  **💪 Empowerment Auditor**: Evaluates rights to education and work. It tracks an "Independence Score" reflecting the user's agency over life choices.
4.  **🤝 NGO Bridge**: Connects users to verified organizations like the Legal Aid Society (LAS) or specialized women's shelters.
5.  **🆘 Helpline Expert**: A mission-critical agent that detects immediate physical danger and provides instant safety protocols and emergency numbers (15, 1094).
6.  **📋 Case Preparer**: Synthesizes the swarm's findings into a clear, actionable roadmap for the user.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🏛️ Legal & Sharia Audits
*   **Nikkah Nama Analysis**: Deep dive into the validity of marriage contracts, registration, and delegated rights (Column 18).
*   **Inheritance Education**: Specific breakdowns of sharia-mandated shares for wives and daughters.
*   **Mobility Guidance**: Clarifying rights to freedom of movement under the Constitution of Pakistan (Article 15) vs. traditional contexts.

### 📊 Real-time Metrics
*   **Protection Score**: A visual representation of current safety levels.
*   **Risk Assessment**: Categorized as Low, Medium, or High (High triggers immediate SOS protocols).
*   **Independence Score**: A metric measuring economic and social agency.

### 💰 Monetization & Sustainability
*   **Professional Promotion**: Integrated slots for verified family lawyers and rights professionals.
*   **NGO Sponsorship**: Visibility for organizations providing free or subsidized legal aid.
*   **Resource Support**: Sponsored workshops and educational content.
